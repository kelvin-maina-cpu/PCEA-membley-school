Our History

Our Journey

The PMS was founded after the Covid ‚Äì 19 world Pandemic. At its inception, the school admitted only 30 learners and 4 workers. As the School Motto suggests; Above and Beyond, the school has since grown tremendously to a complete primary and Junior School registered with the Ministry of Education and managed by the Board of Management.

The school is currently hosting over three hundred learners and thirty-seven members of staff. The main curriculum offered in the school is the Competency Based Education (CBE) National Curriculum. There are several programmes offered in the school that aim at integrating the indoor and outdoor activities fostering holistic development of the learners

The school offers transport services within Ruiru subcounty and other parts of Kiambu county. Other services offered by the school include: balanced meals, clubs and societies, pastoral programmes, indoor board games among many others.

Academic Excellence

Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) | Building Skills for Life

At PCEA Membley School, your child will thrive in Kenya's Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), designed to develop well-rounded learners who can succeed in an ever-changing world. Our approach focuses on:

  • Learner-centered education: Your child at the center of their learning journey
  • Skill development and creativity: Building practical abilities and innovative thinking
  • Critical thinking and problem-solving: Essential skills for future challenges
  • Values-based learning: Integrating moral and ethical development

This ensures your child achieves:

  • Consistent strong performance in internal and external assessments
  • Active participation in academic competitions and exhibitions
  • Holistic learner development through academics, talents, and character

With a low learner-teacher ratio of 1:20, your child receives the individual attention they need to excel in literacy, numeracy, and digital skills.
